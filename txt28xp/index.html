Transfer Instructions

1. Open TI Connect CE software on your computer.

2. Connect your calculator via USB.

3. Drag the downloaded .8xp file into the Calculator Explorer panel.

4. On your calculator, press [PRGM], select your program, and press [ENTER] to view/edit!
